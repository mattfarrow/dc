Roundwood timber construction - Barnkids project

Here is a roundwood timber construction that I designed and constructed for the nursery attended by my children - that's me in the picture with my kids and others from the nursery. Wood is used "in the round" to give a really natural finish.

The building is used as an outdoor shelter in the large garden at the back of the nursery - it's great for young kids as it avoids using wood treated with chemicals.
