About me

I am a carpenter and builder of more than ten yearsâ€™ experience. I ended up here via an unusual route of a politics and philosophy degree, an audio-visual business and a bit of travelling! I have worked on a wide variety of projects from historical barn renovations and conversions to timber frame extensions and interior remodelling.

All my work is informed and influenced by my love of wood as a material and by my desire to minimise the environmental impact of any project I undertake. I have studied for a post-graduate diploma in low-energy architecture at the Centre for Alternative Technology in Machynlleth and maintain my interest in promoting the use of low-energy, natural and locally produced materials.

I currently live with my family in Chiddingfold, not far from where I grew up, and typically work around the Surrey Hills area.
